Our Donate Plan

Donation Plan is a specific plan where-in co-workers would like to donate

their Accrual Plan Balance among themselves.

Food for a family

We must first ensure that every household has access to food. It is very clear during the present flood catastrophe in Sylhet that people put food before all other necessities. For many days, people have been going without food and a means of cooking, which has made them hungry. As a result, using the gift money, our top objective is to feed as many members of the family as we can.

Shelter for a family

Due to this tragedy, many families have lost their houses. They don't have a place to live right now. They went to hurricane shelters or classrooms to find safety. Our second objective is to construct housing for them so that they have a place to live permanently.

Education for a child

It is very understandable that so many young children will drop out of school owing to their poverty in the wake of this calamity. The future of the county is not bright in light of this. To secure their future, we also want to put the donation money into the educational system.

Volunteers

Even if providing food and money is out of the question, you can still support the neighborhood by volunteering your services. We're seeking for volunteers who can help us perform our tasks more efficiently. Together, we can produce something significant and distinctive.
